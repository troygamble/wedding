Getting to Bali & Travel Requirements

Before traveling to Bali for our wedding, please review the current visa requirements, health app instructions, and tourist levies.

Visa & Inoculations

  • Visitor Visa on Arrival (VOA): Approximately $50 AUD per person (including children). Officially this is IDR 500,000. Exchange rates can vary.
  • Check the Official Advisory: Smartraveller – Indonesia for updates on vaccination & entry rules.

SATUSEHAT Health Pass

Indonesia’s free SATUSEHAT app may be required. You can:

  • Register before arrival, or complete it upon landing at the airport.

Customs Declaration

You can submit your customs form online (within 3 days before arrival): ecd.beacukai.go.id . We recommend doing it before you depart.

Tourist Levy

  • Bali periodically discusses a tourist tax levy—often quoted around $15 AUD. The exact policy (“lovebali”) can change. We’ll keep you posted if it becomes mandatory.
