Getting There

You'll arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) in Denpasar, Bali. From there, it's a short and scenic journey to the wedding venue.

  • Distance: Merusaka Nusa Dua is about 14 km from the airport.
  • Travel Time: Around 25-30 minutes by car, depending on traffic.

Transport Options:

  • Taxi / Ride-Share: Use a Bluebird taxi or book a ride via Grab/Gojek (IDR 150,000 – 250,000 / ~$20 AUD).
  • Hotel Transfer: Contact Merusaka for a private pickup service.

Once you arrive, check in, relax!
