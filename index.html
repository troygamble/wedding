Troy & Bec's Wedding

We are so excited to invite you to our wedding celebration in Nusa Dua. Get ready for sun, sand, love, and laughter.

We can’t wait to celebrate with our closest family and friends. Below you’ll find all the important details & schedule of events.

Date & Time: 10th September at 4:00 PM (local Bali time)

Location: Merusaka Hotel, Bali

We’ll be in Bali from 8th - 15th September. Feel free to stay anywhere you like, but we do have a discounted rate at Merusaka if you prefer to stay there.

Attire: Beach formal - light, breezy, and comfortable.

Getting There

You'll arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) in Denpasar, Bali. From there, it's a short and scenic journey to the wedding venue.

  • Distance: Merusaka Nusa Dua is about 14 km from the airport.
  • Travel Time: Around 25-30 minutes by car, depending on traffic.

Transport Options:

  • Taxi / Ride-Share: Use a Bluebird taxi or book a ride via Grab/Gojek (IDR 150,000 – 250,000 / $20 AUD).
  • Hotel Transfer: Contact Merusaka for a private pickup service.
  • Private Driver: Great if you plan to explore Bali. Costs ~IDR 500,000 ($70 AUD) per day.

Once you arrive, check in, relax, and get ready for an unforgettable celebration!

Accommodation

You can book directly with the hotel or choose any other nearby location. To get the discounted rate at Merusaka, use the code WEDTROYREBECCA or mention our wedding when booking.
